# Requirements: A-01 - Coal Combustion Chain

## Executive Summary

Phasing out coal combustion at the pace required for climate targets requires: (1) no new coal plants globally from now; (2) OECD phase-out by 2030, global by 2040; (3) ~$150B/year in clean energy investment above current levels; (4) $30-50B/year in just transition funding; and (5) international cooperation to support developing country transitions. This is technically and economically feasible but requires unprecedented political will.

| Milestone | Date | Requirement |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| No new unabated coal plants | Immediate | Global moratorium |
| Advanced economy phase-out | 2030 | Complete electricity sector exit |
| China coal peak | Already passed | Accelerate decline |
| Global coal power phase-out | 2040 | All unabated plants closed |

| Stop new coal plant approvals | Now | 300+ still in pipeline |
| Begin global capacity decline | 2024-2025 | Mixed signals |

| 90% reduction | 2040 | Requires acceleration |
| Complete phase-out | 2050 | Achievable with action |

## 4. Policy Requirements

### 4.1 Regulatory Framework

| Policy | Requirement | Status |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| No new coal moratorium | Global, immediate | Partial (some countries) |
| Phase-out dates | Legally binding | 40+ countries committed |
| Emissions standards | WHO-aligned | Insufficient globally |
| Carbon pricing | $150+/tonne by 2030 | Far below needed |
| Subsidy elimination | Complete by 2030 | G20 commitment, not delivered |

### 4.2 Financial Regulation

| Policy | Requirement | Status |

| Coal exclusion policies | All public finance | Mostly achieved |
| Prudential regulation | Coal risk weighting | Emerging |
| Fiduciary duty | Include climate risk | Evolving |
| Carbon border adjustment | Major economies | EU CBAM implemented |

### 4.3 International Agreements

## 5. Just Transition Requirements

### 5.1 Worker Support

| Support Type | Requirement | Scale |
|--------------|-------------|-------|
| Wage replacement | 2-5 years at full wage | ~5 million workers globally |
| Retraining programs | Clean energy skills | 80% of displaced workers |
| Early retirement | Age 55+ option | ~30% of workforce |
| Relocation assistance | Regional mobility | Context-specific |
| Healthcare continuation | Until new employment | All displaced workers |

### 5.2 Community Support

| Support Type | Requirement | Scale |
|--------------|-------------|-------|
| Regional development funds | $10-30B/region | 50+ major coal regions |
| Infrastructure investment | Transport, digital, clean energy | All affected regions |
| Economic diversification | New industry attraction | Multi-decade programs |
| Social services | Education, health, housing | Maintained during transition |
| Environmental remediation | Mine site cleanup | 10,000+ sites globally |

### 5.3 Just Transition Finance

| Source | Required Contribution |
|--------|----------------------|
| National governments | 50% of transition costs |
| Regional/state governments | 20% |
| International climate finance | 20% (developing countries) |
| Private sector | 10% |
