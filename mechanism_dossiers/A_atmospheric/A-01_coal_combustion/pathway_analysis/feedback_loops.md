# Feedback Loops: A-01 - Coal Combustion Chain

## Executive Summary

The coal combustion system exhibits multiple reinforcing feedback loops that have driven its 250-year expansion and now impede transition. Key loops include infrastructure lock-in, political capture, and demand induction. Understanding these dynamics reveals leverage points where relatively small interventions can trigger systemic change.
