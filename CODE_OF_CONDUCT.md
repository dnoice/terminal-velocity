- **Intellectual honesty** above ego
- **Evidence** above opinion
- **Precision** above speed
- **Collaboration** above competition
- **The record** above personal credit

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Report violations to: **[maintainer contact - to be added]**

Include:
- Description of the behavior
- Where/when it occurred
- Any relevant links or screenshots

Reports are confidential. Retaliation against reporters is itself a violation.

### Response

Maintainers will:

1. Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. Investigate the report
3. Determine appropriate action
4. Communicate the outcome to relevant parties

### Consequences

Depending on severity:

| Level | Action |
|-------|--------|
| **Warning** | Private notice, expected behavior change |
| **Temporary Ban** | Removal from project spaces for defined period |
| **Permanent Ban** | Permanent removal from all project spaces |
