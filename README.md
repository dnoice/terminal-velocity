## What Is This?

We are not studying "sustainability challenges" or "environmental issues."

We are documenting the **mechanisms** by which an industrial civilization is accelerating toward hard planetary boundaries with full knowledge of the consequences and apparent inability to change course.

Every investigation follows the same logic a medical examiner uses:

1. **Observe the damage** â€” What's dying, degraded, gone
2. **Trace the wound channel** â€” What caused it, through what pathway
3. **Identify the weapon** â€” The specific human activity, system, or incentive
4. **Profile the perpetrator** â€” Who benefits, who decides, who could stop it
5. **Assess intervention difficulty** â€” What would it take to prevent recurrence

**We are not advocates. We are not activists. We are coroners.**

## Evidence Standards

We use a six-tier evidence grading system adapted from clinical medicine:

| Grade | Name | Use |
|-------|------|-----|
| **I** | Direct Measurement | Damage quantification |
| **II** | Official Inventory | Baseline metrics |
| **III** | Validated Model | Projections, scenarios |
| **IV** | Expert Assessment | Context, synthesis |
| **V** | Qualified Report | Supplementary |
| **VI** | Contextual | Background only |

**Damage claims require Grade I-II evidence. No exceptions.**
